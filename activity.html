About AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on creating machines that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include learning from experience, recognizing patterns, solving problems, understanding natural language, and making decisions. AI systems use techniques such as machine learning, deep learning, and neural networks to analyze data and improve their performance over time. As AI continues to evolve, it is becoming an essential part of modern technology, driving innovations in many industries and changing the way people interact with machines.

The uses of AI are vast and rapidly expanding. In healthcare, AI assists doctors by analyzing medical images, predicting diseases, and personalizing treatment plans. In business, it helps automate processes, improve customer service through chatbots, and analyze large datasets to guide decision-making. AI is also transforming industries like transportation, through self-driving cars, and education, by providing personalized learning experiences. Additionally, AI powers virtual assistants, facial recognition, and recommendation systems on platforms like Netflix and Amazon, making everyday life more convenient and efficient.
